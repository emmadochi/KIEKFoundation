About Us

Transforming Lives, One Child at a Time.

The KIEK Foundation is a leading non-governmental organization in Abuja, Nigeria, dedicated to providing quality education, healthcare, and nutrition to underprivileged children.

We believe that every child deserves a fair chance at life. Through our targeted programs and community outreach, we break the cycle of poverty and empower the next generation of African leaders.

Our Vision

A world where every child reaches their full potential.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable education and holistic care.
